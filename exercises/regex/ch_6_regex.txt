> Write a regular expression matching strings of consecutive a and b such as ababab or aba

Regex
(^(a|b){0,1}(ab|ba)*(a|b){0,1}$)

Explanation
begin capture -> (
	start of input -> ^
		then
		of a or b -> (a|b)
			between 0 and 1 -> {0,1}
		then 
		of ab or ba -> (a|b)
			zero or more -> * 
		then
		of a or b -> (a|b)
			between 0 and 1 -> {0, 1}
	end of input -> $
end capture ->)
